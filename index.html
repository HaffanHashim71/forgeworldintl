How We Work

From Enquiry to Delivery

Every order follows a clear, documented process — from the moment you contact us to the day steel arrives at your port.

STEP 01

Enquiry & Specification Review

You send us your requirements — product type, grade, quantity, destination port, and timeline. Our team reviews your specifications, confirms the right grade for your market and environment, and responds with availability and initial pricing within 24 hours.

Product Grade Confirmation Market Certification Check 24h Response
STEP 02

Mill Sourcing & Formal Quote

We approach our certified mill partners to confirm stock availability, production schedules, and pricing for your exact specification. A formal commercial quote is issued with full product details, applicable certifications, and port-to-port lead time.

Mill Stock Verification Formal Proforma Invoice Cert Availability Confirmed
STEP 03

Order Confirmation & Documentation

Once you confirm the order, a purchase agreement and Letter of Credit or advance payment is arranged. We simultaneously prepare all trade documentation — commercial invoice, packing list, Certificate of Origin, and the Mill Test Certificate (EN 10204 Type 3.1).

Purchase Agreement LC / Payment Arrangement MTC · CoO · Invoice
STEP 04

Production & Mill Quality Control

The mill produces or allocates your order to specification. Every heat batch undergoes chemical composition testing, mechanical property verification (yield strength, UTS, elongation), and dimensional checks. Full traceability is maintained from cast number to delivery.

Chemical Composition Test Yield & Tensile Strength Dimensional Check Heat Number Traceability
STEP 05

Pre-Shipment Inspection

An independent third-party inspector — SGS, Bureau Veritas, or Intertek — physically inspects the goods at the mill or warehouse before loading. They verify quantity, dimensions, surface condition, markings, and cross-check against the Mill Test Certificate. A Pre-Shipment Inspection Report is issued.

SGS / BV / Intertek Physical Quantity Check PSI Report Issued
STEP 06

Freight, Loading & Maritime Shipping

Steel is loaded onto the vessel — bulk carrier or container, depending on quantity and destination. We coordinate the freight forwarder, obtain the Bill of Lading, arrange full marine cargo insurance, and send you a complete shipping document package including packing list and B/L copy.

Bill of Lading Marine Cargo Insurance Full Document Package
STEP 07

Port Arrival, Customs & Handover

We support you through customs clearance at your destination port — providing all required documentation for import: Certificate of Origin, MTC, commercial invoice, packing list, and inspection reports. Once cleared, the material is yours. We remain available for any post-delivery technical queries.

Customs Documentation Port Clearance Support Post-Delivery Support
